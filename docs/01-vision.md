# Vision & Positioning

Dreamscape is:
- a creation center
- an art and embodiment space
- a technology + circus + wellness playground

Location:
- Pai, Northern Thailand (mountain town)

Vibe:
- dreamy, unique, playful, immersive
- visually rich and interactive
- should feel like a place you want to explore

Purist constraints:
- vegan
- alcohol-free

Primary audiences:
- Members (regular training and community)
- Workshop students
- Event guests (shows, parties, jams)
- Festival organizers
- Artists, DJs, circus companies
- Retreat leaders, facilitators, resident artists
