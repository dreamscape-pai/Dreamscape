Dreamscape is a multi-use creation center nestled in the mountains of Northern Thailand in Pai. The venue hosts workshops, events, installations, retreats, festivals, residencies, and memberships.

Core idea:
- Dreamy, unique, creative, tech + circus + wellness
- Purist by design: vegan and alcohol-free
- Taglines include:
  - "Everything is Art"
  - "Purist by Design (vegan and alcohol free)"
  - "Technology + Circus + Wellness = Dreamscape"
  - "Dreamscape: Creation Center"
  - "Art and Embodiment space"

Primary goals for the website:
- Attract future members to train
- Attract festivals to run on the property
- Attract DJs/events/workshops/circus companies to perform
- Provide a complete schedule (venue-wide + per-space)
- Enable online payments for memberships, tickets, workshops, etc.
- Provide admin tools and a single source of truth for scheduling
