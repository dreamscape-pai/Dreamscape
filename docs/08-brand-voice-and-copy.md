# Brand Voice & Copy Guidance

Tone:
- dreamy but clear
- welcoming, playful, imaginative
- still practical about schedules, tickets, memberships

Taglines / phrases to incorporate:
- Everything is Art
- Purist by Design (vegan and alcohol free)
- Technology + Circus + Wellness = Dreamscape
- Dreamscape: Creation Center
- Art and Embodiment space

Notes:
- Avoid over-promising.
- Use placeholders where specific policies/prices are unknown.
